GameGlobs is a series of videogame clustering visualizations created at the UC Santa Cruz Center for Games and Playable Media as part of the larger Game Metadata and Citation Project (GAMECIP). Its development was lead by undergragraduate student Timothy Hong and graduate students James Ryan and Eric Kaltman, with professors Noah Wardrip-Fruin and Michael Mateas as their primary advisers. It belongs to a suite of related tools that can all be found here.

For technical details about how GameGlobs works and other information, see our FAQ, or feel free to contact us.

This tool was made possible in part by Institute of Museum and Library Services grant LG-06-13-0205-13.
